How to Effectively Use Excess Material?

Excess wood from construction projects can be repurposed or recycled. It can be used to create furniture, or wooden pallets, or converted into biomass energy. Avoid sending it to landfills by donating it to community projects or recycling it.

Excess concrete can be recycled by crushing it into aggregate, which can be used for new concrete or as a base material for roads. Alternatively, it can be used for landscaping or DIY projects.

Steel is highly recyclable. It can be melted down and reformed into new products with minimal energy compared to producing new steel. It can also be donated or reused for on-site constructions.
